Negotiation tips (your leverage points)

1. Use competing offersApply to 3–4 companies simultaneously. Even a competing phone screen creates negotiating leverage. "I have active conversations at Anthropic and Databricks" can move a Stripe offer by $50K–$100K.
2. Always negotiate stock, not just baseAt Anthropic, Databricks, and Confluent, the real money is in the equity. Ask for a larger initial grant rather than higher base — it's easier for companies to approve and has better upside.
3. New lottery system (2026) works for youLevel IV salaries ($200K+) have a 61% H1B lottery selection rate vs 15% for lower levels. These target companies all pay above that threshold, meaning your H1B odds at premium employers are dramatically better.
4. Cite Levels.fyi data explicitly"Based on current Levels.fyi data, the median for this role and location is $X — I'd like to understand where this offer sits relative to that band." This is now standard and expected.
5. Sign-on bonus as bridge for unvested equityIf you have unvested equity at CreditKarma, ask for a sign-on bonus to cover the cliff. This is standard at all 7 companies and routinely granted.
