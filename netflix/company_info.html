Interview format at Netflix

  • 4–5 rounds: recruiter screen → coding (2x) → system design → culture / values interview ("freedom and responsibility")
  • Coding: Medium–Hard LeetCode. Java or Scala preferred. They care about clean, idiomatic code. Strong emphasis on handling failure cases and edge conditions in distributed systems.
  • System design: Design Netflix's video streaming CDN, a distributed cache invalidation system, or a high-throughput event processing pipeline. Think scale: 300M users, 10M RPS.
  • Culture round: Netflix's "freedom and responsibility" culture is real. They hire adults, give autonomy, and expect ownership. Show you make decisions independently and don't need hand-holding. Read the Netflix Culture Deck before interviewing.
  • Key differentiator: Netflix values engineers who can pick up any tech independently. Your Scala, TypeScript, Java, Kotlin, Python + multi-domain background across 10 companies is strong evidence of learning agility.
  • H1B: Confirmed active sponsor. Median H1B salary $226K (FY2025). Confirm at recruiter screen.
