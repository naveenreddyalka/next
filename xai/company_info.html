How to crack xAI

  • Interview style: Fast, lean loop. Less process than FAANG. Depth and speed matter more than polish.
  • Rounds: 2-3 total: technical screen (1hr) -> virtual loop (2-3 hrs) coding + system design + culture.
  • Coding: Medium-Hard LeetCode plus practical system thinking. May skew practical over pure LC.

4-week prep plan:

  • 2 weeks focused LC: 40 Medium, 15 Hard (arrays, graphs, system design basics)
  • Prepare end-to-end system stories: MakeMyTrip payments + CK platform framework
  • Practice whiteboarding a rate-limited API in 45 min
  • Read about Grok product and xAI mission before culture chat
  • Emphasize ownership, speed, and shipping under ambiguity
