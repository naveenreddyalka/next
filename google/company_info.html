Interview format at Google

  • 5–7 rounds: recruiter → coding phone screen → 4–5 onsite rounds (coding, system design, behavioral, Googleyness)
  • Coding: Hard LeetCode. Highest bar of all 7 companies. Graphs, trees, DP, strings. Do 100+ problems before interviewing. Use NeetCode.io roadmap.
  • System design: Design Google Maps at scale, a distributed search index, or a global payments system. Think about sharding, consistency, latency at Google's size.
  • Googleyness round: Behavioral + culture fit. Google values intellectual humility, collaboration, and comfort with ambiguity. Prepare 5 STAR stories.
  • Timeline: Apply now — Google's process takes 2–4 months. Start this one early, in parallel with faster-moving companies.
  • H1B: #1 volume sponsor in the US (1,050+ FY2025 approvals). Dedicated immigration team. Most reliable H1B sponsor of all 7 targets. Confirm with recruiter upfront.
  • Leveling: Push for L5. If they suggest L4, negotiate — your 17 years + MS CS should land L5. L5 = Senior SWE = your target.
