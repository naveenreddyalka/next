Why Google

Senior Software Engineer at intersection of cloud infrastructure and financial systems. MS CS, ASU 2021.

Company snapshot

  • Company type: Public (NASDAQ: GOOGL). Mega-cap, highly liquid RSUs. Most predictable equity.
  • Salary range (Bay Area): L5 SWE Bay Area: $400K-$500K TC; L6 Staff $700K-$1.2M (Levels.fyi 2026).

Public RSUs with refresh. L5->L6 promotion is the path to $700K+.

Benefits and perks

  • Free meals at Bay Area campuses (famous perk)
  • RSU grants + semi-annual refresh
  • 401(k) match + mega backdoor Roth options
  • ESPP discount
  • Excellent medical/dental/vision
  • Generous parental leave (18-22 weeks)
  • On-site gyms, massages, shuttles (campus-dependent)

How to crack Google

  • Interview style: Classic Google loop. Hardest LC bar in this list. Googleyness behavioral round.
  • Rounds: 5-7: recruiter -> phone screen -> 4-5 onsite (coding, system design, Googleyness, team match).
  • Coding: Hard LeetCode only. 100+ problems recommended. Graphs, DP, strings are core.

4-week prep plan:

  • 6-8 weeks: NeetCode 150 + 50 extra Hard (start early - longest timeline)
  • System design: Google-scale search index, Maps sharding, global payments
  • Prep 5 Googleyness STAR stories (humility, collaboration, ambiguity)
  • Negotiate level upfront: push L5 with 17 yrs + MS CS
  • Target Cloud AI or Google Pay teams specifically
  • Use interview warmup on leetcode.com/google top questions
