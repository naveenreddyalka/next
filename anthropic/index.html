Why Anthropic

Staff-level Software Engineer with 17+ years building platform infrastructure and distributed backend systems. At Credit Karma, designed and own the Portal Provider framework relied on by 90%+ of portals clients. MS CS, ASU 2021.

Company snapshot

  • Company type: Private (venture-backed). Last valued ~$60B+ (2025). No IPO announced; high growth potential if AI market holds.
  • Salary range (Bay Area): Senior SWE Bay Area: $450K-$650K TC typical; Staff/Lead $700K-$890K+ (Levels.fyi 2026).

Fastest-growing frontier AI lab after OpenAI. Equity upside if IPO or secondary liquidity events.

Benefits and perks

  • Premium medical/dental/vision

How to crack Anthropic

  • Interview style: Structured loop, high bar. Mix of hard coding, infra/system design, and values alignment.
  • Rounds: 5-6: recruiter screen -> 2x coding -> system design -> infra/platform design -> values/culture.
  • Coding: Heavy LeetCode (Hard). Also distributed-systems flavored problems. Less pure puzzle, more real-world constraints at Staff level.

4-week prep plan:

  • 4-6 weeks LeetCode: NeetCode 150 + 30 Hard (graphs, DP, concurrency)
  • Prep 3 system designs: deployment pipeline, service mesh, observability platform
  • Prep 2 infra designs: service registry, multi-tenant auth at scale
  • Read Anthropic core views + Responsible Scaling Policy
  • Practice Finagle EOL + 100% uptime modernization story (5 min + deep dive)
  • Mock 1 values round: AI safety, long-term impact, intellectual humility
