Interview format at Uber

  • 4–5 rounds: recruiter screen → coding (2x) → system design → hiring manager / bar raiser
  • Coding: Medium LeetCode. Java or Go preferred. They care about correctness, handling edge cases, and thinking through failure scenarios in distributed systems.
  • System design: Design Uber's surge pricing system, a distributed rate limiter, or a real-time dispatch matching platform. Think high-throughput, low-latency, fault tolerance.
  • Uber values: ownership, moving fast, data-driven decisions. Show you can own a system end-to-end and make independent architectural decisions.
  • Level negotiation: Push for Senior or Senior Staff — don't accept SWE II regardless of initial offer. Your 17 years of experience should be Senior at minimum.
  • H1B: Large company, established process. Confirm sponsorship at recruiter screen.
